# Backlink Execution Plan (2026-02-21)

## Weekly Cadence
1. Build a list of 30 prospects from one target type.
2. Send 10 personalized pitches per day for 3 days.
3. Publish one support asset (guide/checklist/case study) each week.
4. Follow up after 5 business days and 12 business days.

## Target Classes
- **T1** | Parenting media and family productivity publishers: Data-backed household operations frameworks. Asset: /task-systems and /chore-systems hubs. Offer: Expert contribution + actionable checklist resource.
- **T1** | Meal planning and nutrition editorial sites: Meal planning + grocery automation system. Asset: /meal-plans + /grocery-lists hubs. Offer: Guest guide with practical planning templates.
- **T1** | Fitness and training blogs/newsletters: Family-friendly workout + macro integration. Asset: /workout-tracking + /macro-plans hubs. Offer: Cross-over article for parents who train.
- **T2** | School/PTA newsletters and community family resources: Household planning templates for busy school-week routines. Asset: /household-templates + /task-systems. Offer: Free printable checklist + resource page mention.
- **T2** | Local family/lifestyle blogs: Weekly home systems to reduce family stress. Asset: /resources. Offer: Co-branded local family workflow article.
- **T2** | Wellness newsletters and creators: Lifestyle tracking frameworks for sleep and habits. Asset: /lifestyle-tracking. Offer: Newsletter-ready practical habit framework.
- **T3** | Template roundups and productivity tool directories: Operational templates with real execution flow. Asset: /household-templates and /chore-systems. Offer: Free template pack listing.
- **T3** | Podcast hosts in parenting, productivity, and wellness: How families build one operational system across meals, tasks, and health. Asset: /resources. Offer: Founder interview + audience toolkit.
